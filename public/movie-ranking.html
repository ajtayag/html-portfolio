The Best Movies According to Jose Tayag

My Top 3 Movies of All- Time.

Home Alone

Publication Year: 1990
"Home Alone" is a family comedy film directed by Chris Columbus. It tells the story of Kevin McCallister, an 8-year-old boy who is accidentally left behind when his family goes on a Christmas vacation to Paris. Kevin must defend his home from two burglars using a series of booby traps and clever tricks.

Home Alone 2: Lost in New York

Publication Year: 1992
In this sequel to "Home Alone," Kevin McCallister finds himself alone again, but this time he accidentally boards the wrong flight and ends up in New York City while his family is in Florida for the holidays. Kevin once again faces off against the same burglars, this time in the Big Apple, using his wits and creativity to outsmart them.

The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring

Publication Year: 2001
The Fellowship of the Ring" is the first installment in the epic fantasy trilogy based on J.R.R. Tolkien's novels. The film follows the journey of Frodo Baggins and a diverse group of companions as they set out to destroy a powerful ring that could bring about the end of the world. They face numerous challenges and enemies along the way, including the dark lord Sauron.
